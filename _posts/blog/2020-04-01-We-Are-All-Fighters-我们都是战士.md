<div>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>This is a war,</p>
  <p>这是一场战争</p>
  <p>and we are all fighters.</p>
  <p>而我们都是战士！</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>To the fighters</p>
  <p>致敬所有的战士们！</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>We are facing a dark time right now.</p>
  <p>我们正面临着黑暗时刻</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Some of us are stuck at home</p>
  <p>我们中有些人不得不禁足在家</p>
  <p>Some are stuck far away from home</p>
  <p>有些人不得不滞留他乡</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Some of us have lost their jobs，</p>
  <p>我们中有人失去了工作</p>
  <p>Some have to shut down their businesses</p>
  <p>有人关停了赖以为生的生意</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Some of us are cutting down the expenses</p>
  <p>我们中有人不得不缩减开支</p>
  <p>Some can’t even pay their rent and bills</p>
  <p>有人甚至支付不起房租和账单</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Some&nbsp;can’t go back to school</p>
  <p>我们中有人无法回到学校</p>
  <p>Some can’t get back to their jobs</p>
  <p>有人无法回到工作岗位</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>And some of us are still working day and night,</p>
  <p>我们中有人必须日夜坚守</p>
  <p>no matter how exhausted they are</p>
  <p>无论多么疲惫</p>
  <p>Or how dangerous it is.</p>
  <p>抑或多么危险</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Some of us have become infected.</p>
  <p>我们中有人被感染</p>
  <p>They were isolated from their families, their loved ones,</p>
  <p>他们不得不离开家人和所爱之人</p>
  <p>and have to be alone in the wards,</p>
  <p>独自在病房面对恐惧</p>
  <p>Or even worse, it is their children, parents, or loved ones that are infected</p>
  <p>甚至更遭的是，被感染的是他们的孩子、父母或者爱人</p>
  <p>and they don’t even have a chance to say goodbye</p>
  <p>他们甚至来不及当面道别</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>The virus is rampaging</p>
  <p>病毒在大行肆虐</p>
  <p>The numbers are increasing</p>
  <p>感染人数在不断增加</p>
  <p>The rumors are spreading</p>
  <p>谣言四起</p>
  <p>The fear is growing</p>
  <p>恐惧弥漫</p>
  <p>It seems that all of a sudden</p>
  <p>似乎整个国家都在一瞬间</p>
  <p>the whole country lost its vitality and prosperity.</p>
  <p>失去了往日的活力和繁华</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>You are not the only one who is worrying,</p>
  <p>你不是一个人在担忧</p>
  <p>you are not the only one who is fearing,</p>
  <p>你不是一个人在恐惧</p>
  <p>You are not the only one who is struggling</p>
  <p>你不是一个人在苦苦支撑</p>
  <p>We are all facing a dark time right now,</p>
  <p>我们都在面临一个黑暗时刻</p>
  <p>Our country is facing a dark time right now.</p>
  <p>我们整个国家都在面临一个黑暗时刻</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>This is a war!</p>
  <p>这是一场战争！</p>
  <p>It’s a war without guns, bombs, or smoke</p>
  <p>这是一场没有枪、没有炮、没有硝烟的战争</p>
  <p>But it’s a war with virus, doubts, fears, rumors, and discriminations.</p>
  <p>但这是一场与病毒、怀疑、恐惧、谣言和歧视的战争</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>But we shall all be fighters, my dear fellows!</p>
  <p>但我们都是战士！我亲爱的同胞们，</p>
  <p>Not just the doctors, the nurses, the policemen and the scientists who should fight</p>
  <p>不仅仅只是医生、护士、警察和科学家在战斗</p>
  <p>All of us should be the fighters in this war</p>
  <p>我们都应该成为这场战争中的战士</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>We shall fight against fear</p>
  <p>我们应该和恐惧斗争</p>
  <p>We shall fight against uncertainty</p>
  <p>我们应该和未知斗争</p>
  <p>We shall fight against doubts</p>
  <p>我们应该和怀疑斗争</p>
  <p>We shall fight against selfishness</p>
  <p>我们应该和自私斗争</p>
  <p>We shall fight against rumors</p>
  <p>我们应该和谣言斗争</p>
  <p>We shall fight against discriminations</p>
  <p>我们应该和歧视斗争</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>This is going to be a fierce and cruel war</p>
  <p>这将会是一场激烈的严酷的战争</p>
  <p>But don’t be afraid,</p>
  <p>但是不用害怕</p>
  <p>We’re going to win this war together in the end!</p>
  <p>我们终将取得这场战争的胜利</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Our people may get sick,</p>
  <p>我们的人民可能生病</p>
  <p>our city may get sick,</p>
  <p>我们的城市可能生病</p>
  <p>but we as a brave nation, will never ever get sick</p>
  <p>但我们作为一个勇敢的民族，绝不会被病魔打倒</p>
  <p>Because we have the history, spirit and determination to win.</p>
  <p>因为我们拥有必胜的历史、精神和决心！</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>We’ve been on this planet for over 5000 years.</p>
  <p>我们在地球上已经延续了5000年</p>
  <p>We’ve witnessed much darker moments</p>
  <p>我们见证过更加黑暗的时刻</p>
  <p>We’ve been though much crueler trials</p>
  <p>我们经历过更加残酷的考验</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>No matter it’s the war, invasion, starvation, poverty or natural&nbsp;disasters</p>
  <p>无论是战争、侵略、饥饿、贫穷还是自然灾害</p>
  <p>We’ve always survived and</p>
  <p>我们总是幸存下来</p>
  <p>We’ve always stood back up on our feet</p>
  <p>我们总是能重新站立起来</p>
  <p>We’ve always won in the end.</p>
  <p>我们总是赢的最后的胜利</p>
  <p>And we will definitely win this time.</p>
  <p>所以这一次我们也将取得最终的胜利</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>We don’t know how long this will take,</p>
  <p>我们不知道这场战争将持续多久</p>
  <p>Or what cost we have to pay</p>
  <p>又或者我们将付出什么代价</p>
  <p>But there’s one thing we are 100% sure</p>
  <p>但有一件事我们100%的确定</p>
  <p>That is: we are going to win this war in the end!</p>
  <p>那就是我们终将赢得这场战争的胜利</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>Because that’s what we’ve been doing for the past 5000 years.</p>
  <p>因为在我们过去5000年的历史中，我们就是这样做的</p>
  <p>And that’s what makes us the greatest&nbsp;nation ever</p>
  <p>而正因如此成就了我们这个世上最伟大的民族</p>
  <p>It is only by going through dark times that we can separate&nbsp;the greatest from the great.</p>
  <p>只有经历黑暗时刻才能区分伟大和平庸</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>In each dark time,</p>
  <p>在每一个黑暗时刻</p>
  <p>We’ve always fought together</p>
  <p>我们总是共同抗争</p>
  <p>No matter how strong our enemy was</p>
  <p>无论我们的敌人有多么强大</p>
  <p>Or how&nbsp;impossible&nbsp;it seemed to win</p>
  <p>又或者我们赢的概率微乎其微</p>
  <p>We just kept fighting</p>
  <p>我们都会坚持战斗</p>
  <p>Because we are all fighters!</p>
  <p>因为我们都是战士！</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
  <p>When all of this is over,</p>
  <p>当所有这一切结束后，</p>
  <p>we’ll have another great and heroic story</p>
  <p>我们又有了一个伟大的英雄的故事</p>
  <p>to tell our children, our grandchildren</p>
  <p>可以讲给我们的子孙听</p>
  <p>and&nbsp;they will tell the next generations.</p>
  <p>而他们也将把这个故事告诉给他们的下一代</p>
  <p>That’s how we’ll pass this great spirit on,</p>
  <p>我们会将传递这份伟大的精神代代相传</p>
  <p>and keep it alive forever!</p>
  <p>永存不朽!</p>
  <p>&nbsp;</p>
